City on the water

The capital of Tunga is a unique and captivating place. The contrast between the bustling cityscape and the calm waters creates a striking and unforgettable scene. The water can provide recreational opportunities such as kayaking and sailing while offering great views.

Local festivals

Local island festivals can be a vibrant and exciting celebration of a community's unique culture and traditions. These festivals showcase local arts, crafts, music, dance and cuisine, providing visitors with an authentic and immersive experience.

ZOO

A visit to the zoo can be a fun and educational experience for people of all ages. Seeing exotic and familiar animals up close can be an exciting and unforgettable experience. Children in particular can enjoy learning about different species, their habitats, behaviors and adaptations.

Mountain trips!

Mountain hiking can be an exciting and rewarding experience for those who love nature and adventure. The rugged terrain, high peaks and fresh mountain air create a scenic and inspiring environment for hiking, camping and other outdoor activities.

Traditional fishing..

This type of unique fishing can be a rewarding experience that connects people with the natural world and the cultural heritage of their community. These methods may include using handmade nets, fishing from wooden boats or kayaks, or even scuba diving for shellfish.
