must see!

Tropical forests

A visit to the forest can be a unique and unforgettable experience. While exploring the rich flora and fauna, from tall trees to colorful birds and elusive mammals. The sounds of the forest create an immersive environment that is both serene and exciting.

Lovely old town

The old town can take you back in time to a bygone era. Wandering the narrow streets and alleys, you can soak up the history and culture of this place. The architecture, often characterized by ornate facades and intricate details, speaks of the craftsmanship and artistry of the past.

Breathtaking beach

The beaches of Bora can charm you with the natural beauty of the world. Crystal clear water, soft sand and swaying palm trees create a serene and peaceful environment, perfect for relaxing and rejuvenating.

Off-road races!

Off-road racing can be an exciting adrenaline-filled experience. Wide open spaces and varied terrain, including dunes and rocky outcrops, provide drivers with a challenging and exciting route. The salty sea air and stunning ocean views create a picturesque backdrop for an exciting race.

Diving in the ocean..

As you descend into the deep blue waters, you can encounter a whole range of sea creatures, from shoals of colorful fish to graceful sea turtles and majestic manta rays. The vibrant colors of coral reefs and underwater plants create a surreal and different environment that is both beautiful and peaceful.
